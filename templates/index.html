Algerian Forest Fire Prediction

Welcome to the Forest Fire Prediction System

This project aims to predict the likelihood of forest fires in different regions of Algeria. By analyzing key factors such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and rainfall, we can provide timely fire risk assessments to help mitigate disasters and protect the environment.

Explore the data, learn about fire patterns in the region, and discover the tools used to make accurate predictions in this critical field.
